simplebox.js is a small jQuery plugin (1400 bytes) that is easy to modify or extend to fit your needs. Abstracting your font-sizes into simple single purpose classes targeting individual breakpoints can help improve your sites css performance and reduce code bloat. Adjusting font-sizes and designing around content in the browser becomes easy as sunday morning.
